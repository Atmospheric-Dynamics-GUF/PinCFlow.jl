Authors

PinCFlow.jl's development is coordinated by a group of principal developers, who are also its main contributors and who can be contacted in case of questions about PinCFlow. In addition, there are contributors who have provided substantial additions or modifications. Together, these two groups form "The PinCFlow.jl Authors" as mentioned under License.
